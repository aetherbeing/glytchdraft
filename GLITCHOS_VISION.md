# GlitchOS Vision

## INTERIOR FLOOR STACK
- Ground Floor: public lobby, storefront media surface
- Floor 2: Gallery - art, images, video walls
- Floor 3: Cinema - TikTok-style geo-tagged video room
- Floor 4: Salon - video conferencing, spatial audio
- Floor 5: Studio - creator workspace, geo-tagged archive
- Floor 6: Penthouse - private, maximum customization
- Roof: public terrace, billboard, city-facing broadcast

## INSIDE/OUTSIDE DICHOTOMY
- Exterior = public signal, broadcast, ambient presence
- Interior = private territory, controlled access
- Privacy levels: street / lobby / room / private
- Trace cost scales with interiority and control

## BUILDING HIERARCHY
- Tier 1: Landmark/iconic - high Trace, platform showcase
- Tier 2: Prime commercial - business-facing
- Tier 3: Neighborhood commercial - local identity
- Tier 4: Residential - personal, intimate
- Reserved: civic, government, hospitals - unclaimed layer

## PLACE-INDEXED MEDIA
- Content geo-tagged to specific buildings and floors
- Cinema floor aggregates content tagged to that location
- Genuinely new content format - nobody has built this
